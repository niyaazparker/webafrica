Frequently asked qustions

Q. Can I update my own content?

Yes, via your website’s easy-to-use content management system. We will supply you with all these details during process.

Q. Do I have to choose an Add-on?

No, you can choose one now or add it later on when you decide to take your website to the next level.

Q.Is my website optimised for Google?

Yes. Your website has been designed to do well on Google (and other search engines) but to take it to the next level we suggest you take our Searcher Add-on package.

Q.What is SEO?

SEO - Search Engine Optimization - is the process of getting traffic from the “free”, “organic”, “editorial” or “natural” listings on search engines. It is free as in that you don’t need to pay to be ranked in the search engines.
